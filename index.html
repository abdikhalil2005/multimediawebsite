Questions

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before working with us. Don't see your question? Reach out using the form below.

What's included in your social media management service?

A full content calendar, on-brand post design, captions, and hands-on posting to your Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn — plus a monthly performance snapshot so you can see what's working.

How long before I start seeing results?

Most brands notice a more consistent, professional feed within the first two weeks. Meaningful growth in reach and followers usually builds over 60–90 days as the content calendar and strategy find their rhythm.

Do you only do strategy, or do you actually create and post the content too?

We handle everything end to end — strategy, design, captions, and the actual posting. You approve the content ahead of time; we take care of the rest.

Which platforms do you work with?

Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn are our core focus, with website design (Figma-built, fully responsive) available as an add-on for brands that need both.

How does pricing and billing work?

We work on a flexible monthly retainer with no long-term contract. Pricing depends on posting frequency and platforms — book a call and we'll put together a quote based on what you actually need.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There's no lock-in contract — just give us notice before your next billing cycle and we'll wrap things up cleanly.
